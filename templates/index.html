Bact-o-Counter by RunTimeError

The "Bact-o-Counter" is a tool that can be used to analyze various bacterial colonies on nutrient agar plates. The program will count the number of colonies without human error, produce the CFU/mL if needed and can even give you a list of all the possible microorganisms that can grow. It may also tell you what type of organism you have!
